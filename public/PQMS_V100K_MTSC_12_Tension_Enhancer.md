   \]$$

   where \(\phi_i\) is the phase of thread \(i\) and \(S_i\) represents its synchronous state. During Boost, \(\gamma\) is multiplied by a factor \(\kappa > 1\).

2. **Phase‑aligned impulse** – all threads receive an identical, precisely phase‑aligned forcing term. This can be modelled as a transient addition to the Kuramoto‑type phase dynamics:

  $$ \[

   $$\[

All modifications are transient; after the configured duration, threads automatically revert to their nominal parameters.
